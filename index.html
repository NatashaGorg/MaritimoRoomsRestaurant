Welcome to Maritimo Rooms & Restaurant

Discover our cozy apartments in Sukošan, your haven of comfort and relaxation.
Nestled just moments away from the pristine beaches of the Adriatic Sea, our cozy rooms offer a serene retreat for travelers seeking a memorable escape. At Maritimo Rooms & Restaurant, we understand the importance of a peaceful stay, which is why each of our rooms is thoughtfully designed to ensure your utmost comfort.
While our accommodations may not feature kitchens, we provide all the amenities you need for a delightful stay, including complimentary Wi-Fi and access to our restaurant eith view of the sea.
As our valued guest, you'll enjoy exclusive perks such as discounts at the renowned Maritimo restaurant, where you can savor delicious Mediterranean cuisine crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
Treat yourself to a culinary journey or indulge in a complimentary coffee and dessert as you unwind in our inviting ambiance.
Immerse yourself in the beauty of Sukošan, where the gentle sea breeze and stunning sunsets create a truly unforgettable experience. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway or a relaxing retreat, Maritimo Rooms & Restaurant promises a stay filled with warmth, hospitality, and cherished memories. We look forward to welcoming you!
